What's the point?

Connect with EVERYONE you know who plays

Nearly 60 million people1 are expected to play fantasy sports in 2017! With so many people playing there are lots of opportunities to play with different groups of people, but every additional league takes time (and possibly money). As a result most people miss out on playing with some group of people whether it's childhood friends, extended family, workplace colleagues, new friends, etc. Fantasy Football Network will allow you to connect with anyone you like. Once you connect you can

  • Get weekly updates on how their teams did
  • Receive notifications of trades and moves they make
  • See the status in their current leagues
  • View every team they have ever managed

Compete against anyone with NO ADDITIONAL EFFORT

Play and manage your fantasy teams as your normally would, and Fantasy Football Network will score you on how well you did. Our algorithms work independent of the type of league or scoring system in place. We factor in things like:

  • Whether you won the match up
  • How your teams actual performance compared to projections
  • How your opponents' teams actual performance compared to projections
  • Players available on the waiver wire
  • Your team's score compared to the rest of your league
  • And much more!
